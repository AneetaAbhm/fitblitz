Fitness Daily Challenges

Weight Gaining Plan

30-Day Plan

Perform weightlifting exercises for 30 minutes every day.

Consume an additional 500 calories per day through nutrient-dense foods.

Incorporate protein shakes into your post-workout routine.

15-Day Plan

Increase your daily caloric intake by 300 calories.

Focus on compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses.

Aim for at least 8 hours of sleep to aid muscle recovery.

5-Day Plan

Consume a protein-rich meal within 30 minutes of completing your workout.

Incorporate healthy fats into your diet, such as avocados and nuts.

Track your progress and adjust your calorie intake accordingly.

Weight Losing Plan

30-Day Plan

Engage in cardiovascular exercises for at least 45 minutes every day.

Limit your daily calorie intake by 500 to create a calorie deficit.

Incorporate high-intensity interval training (HIIT) into your workout routine.

15-Day Plan

Include more vegetables and lean proteins in your meals.

Reduce your consumption of processed and sugary foods.

Stay consistent with your exercise routine and aim for at least 10,000 steps per day.

5-Day Plan

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.

Incorporate strength training exercises to maintain muscle mass.

Keep a food diary to track your calorie intake and identify areas for improvement.
